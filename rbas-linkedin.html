Strategy & Guidelines

Key principles and a checklist to ensure consistent, high-quality posting.

Content Principles

  • Professional & Pragmatic: Maintain a technically precise, safety-forward voice. Avoid marketing hype.
  • Audience-Centric: Address the needs of contractors, fleet managers, and dealers with practical insights.
  • Visual Consistency: Use the specified image theme with silhouetted operators to build a strong, brand-safe visual identity.
  • India-First, Global-Ready: Use Indian English but ensure content is easily understood by a global audience.
  • Value-Driven CTAs: Align calls-to-action with the funnel stage, from awareness ("Learn more") to lead-gen ("Enquire now").

Posting Checklist

  • Double-check Asia/Kolkata times; schedule at least 24h in advance.
  • Proofread for Indian English, technical accuracy, and safety-first messaging.
  • Use 4–8 relevant hashtags; avoid banned/overly generic tags.
  • Ensure image renders show PPE; no faces/logos/readable text.
  • Link to website contact page; add UTM parameters to all outbound links.
  • Engage with comments promptly to foster community.
  • Monitor post-performance and adapt strategy based on what resonates.
